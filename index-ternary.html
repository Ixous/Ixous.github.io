Ternary Computer

I first learnt about ternary computers in July 2015. Since then, I've been fascinated by this atypical, elegant and potentially under-estimated alternative to binary computing. Starting from the most fundamental rules of logic of this new algebra, my goal is to explore the feasibility and advantages of ternary processors.
